Strategic Communications Subcommittee Report
Date: 6/15/16
Committee: Strategic Communications
Chair: Candice deCcsipkes
In attendance: Kate Brown, Candice deCcsipkes, Mike Lafortune, Lindsay Sterling, Valy Steverlynck
Meeting Date: 6/15/16
Agenda Items and Discussion:
1. Exit Survey: The group made the changes suggested by the Board. Discussed "how" the survey
should be distributed -- when and by what means (Survey Monkey, Google)? Mike was going to talk
to Ray Grogan about how he did his recent survey. These questions will be taken to the Board. The
survey will be taken to the Board for final approval of the draft and then sent to administrators for
their input.
2. Satisfaction Survey: The group reviewed five sample surveys, choosing items to be considered for
addition and/or wording for the RSU5 survey. Group decided on topic sections for the survey:
Academics, Communication, Student Support, Extra-Curricular Activities and Sports, Behavior,
Facilities, School Culture and Climate. Candy will take suggestions and revise draft for the next
meeting.
