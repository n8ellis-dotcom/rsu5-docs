ii. Allows for largest field space.
iii. One continuous curve – better for runners.
iv. Advantage – long jump, triple jump will be inside of the track; pole
vault would be more isolated which is good.
v. Turf area same; track area is less. Virtually about the same; cost
would be similar; redesign would need to occur.

1. This could delay construction start.
