Finance Subcommittee Report
Date: March 28, 2019
Committee: Finance Committee

In attendance: Kate Brown, Michelle Ritcheson, John Morang, Becky Foley
Absent: Michelle Lickteig
Meeting Date: March 27, 2019
Agenda Items and Discussion:
Next Steps on Cost Sharing:
The cost sharing discussion continued. The committee is leaning towards using a combination of pupil
count and valuation as the two factors to determine the formula. Michelle Ritcheson shared a chart that
she created using those two variables. Pros and cons were discussed of the various options. There was
also discussion about the new formula being used for the FY 21 budget, not this upcoming year as there
needs to be time to present to the Board for deliberation.
The next two meetings were scheduled for 5:00 p.m. on April 10th at Durham Community School and
April 24th at Pownal Elementary School
