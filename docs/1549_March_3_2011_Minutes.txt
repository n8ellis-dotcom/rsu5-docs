Agenda Items and Discussion:
Strategic Framework Report Out Planning:
The committee met with Ken Templeton from Great Schools to review a draft of the information for
our upcoming State of the Schools Report. The committee finalized the text and graphs for this report
of our progress against the indicators outlined in the Strategic Framework. The report will be mailed to
every household in our three communities. The document should arrive at households during the week
of March 21st.
The committee also discussed the RSU5 Education Forum scheduled for March 29, 2011 at 6:30 pm in
the FPAC. Academic and financial indicators outlined in the Strategic Framework will be used to
provide an accounting of our performance at this point in time. The Forum will provide a community
discussion of what’s been accomplished, areas to celebrate, and what goals we should establish as we
look to the future. Invitations will be extended through the “Delivering on the Mission message,
school newsletters, the town cable stations, and through press releases.
