Finance Committee Minutes
February 28, 2024
Freeport High School Library
In Attendance: Beth Munsen Chair, Maura Pillsbury, Michelle Ritcheson, Jean
Skorapa,Superintendent, Kelly Wentworth, Director of Finance & HR
Chair Munsen called the meeting to order at 5:03 P.M.
FY25 Proposed Budget Review:
Kelly reviewed the FY23 Estimated Undesignated Fund Balance figures. Kelly presented Title 20-A;
Section 15689-B (6) which states that the district is allowed 9% in unallocated fund balances in both
2023-24 and 2024-25. By setting up the newly proposed article reserves and contributing to existing
reserve accounts, we will be in compliance with that law.
Other:
FY23 Audit is in its final stages. FY24 Audit Fieldwork has been scheduled for September 16th - 20th
with follow up work dates on October 15th - 18th if needed.
Meeting adjourned at 6:01 P.M.
