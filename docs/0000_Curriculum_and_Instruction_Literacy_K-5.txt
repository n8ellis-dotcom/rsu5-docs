﻿K-5 Literacy Curriculum 
2025-2026
RSU No. 5  is a district that is responsive to the diverse learning needs of students. We have updated both the reading and writing units in K-2 and writing units in grades 3-5 over the past 2 years to ensure students participate in a comprehensive Literacy Program across their experience in our district. This year we will be using the updated reading units in grades 3-5. Our curricula include explicit instruction in phonological awareness, phonics and word recognition, fluency, vocabulary, oral language comprehension, encoding, writing processes and text comprehension. The updates in our curriculums have drawn from a wide body of peer reviewed research in both the science of literacy development and effective instructional practices.
