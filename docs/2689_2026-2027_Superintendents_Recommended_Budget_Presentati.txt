Rationale for Tier 2 (~7%)– Key Impacts
● Elementary–
○ Fewest reductions at this level where individual student needs are great
○ Maintains core programming
● Middle-
○ New FMS Schedule Allows More Time for Social Studies
○ FMS Unified Arts Dilemma and Decision
○ Reducing office staff maintains consistency with other schools
● Secondary-
○ Class sizes allow for reduction in Math
○ Maintain offerings in all three existing World Languages
○ Reducing the printing clerk and community outreach maintains core
capabilities, and preserves core instructional programs
