NEPN/NSBA Code: BEDL
ADJOURNMENT OF BOARD MEETINGS
Meetings of the Board of Directors shall be adjourned by 10:00 p.m. except when a motion to
continue past this hour has been carried by a two-thirds (2/3) vote of the Board members in
attendance. It is also intended that a topic started before the 10:00 p.m. curfew may be continued
beyond that point.
