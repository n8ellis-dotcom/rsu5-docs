Finance Committee Minutes

Minutes
1. Meeting called to order at 5:13 p.m.
2. FY26 Proposed Budget Review -
a. Superintendent Skorapa reviewed a list of Board member budget questions that had
been compiled to date. Committee members made some suggestions and
clarifications that will help with the creation of informative materials for the full Board’s
budget deliberations.
3. FY24 Audit Update -
a. The Finance Director reported that Marge, the Auditor, had reached out with a few
final questions/clarifications and was preparing the draft for review. The draft is
expected the last week of February for the Finance Director to review. We will ask the
Auditor to review the audit at our April Finance meeting.
4. Other -
a. Next meeting of the Finance Committee, March 26, 2025 @ FHS. Ms. Ritcheson was
asked to share the previous cost sharing analysis spreadsheet so that it could be
updated and reviewed at the next meeting of the Finance Committee.
Meeting adjourned at 5:57 P.M.
