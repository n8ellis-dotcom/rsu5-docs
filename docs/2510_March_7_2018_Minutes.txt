Strategic Planning Advisory Committee
March 7, 2018
Minutes
Committee Members Present:
Becky Foley, Julie Nickerson, Rhea Fitzpatrick, Valy Steverlynck, Lindsay Sterling, Stephanie
Paine, Jonathan Dawson, Seth Koenig, Cynthia Alexander, Nancy Drolet
The Committee met with Mary Jane McCalmon. After establishing norms, the committee began
to discuss the steps for the strategic planning process. Mary Jane passed out a timeline that
includes having the strategic planning completed by May of 2019 although it could be finished
prior to that date.
Discussion centered around having the entire strategic plan revisited, including the mission and
vision.
Documents were presented showing the process that would be utilized. The group decided to
use a consensus model for decision-making. Input will be gathered from various stakeholders
this spring.
Next meeting will be March 15, followed by March 21, and April 4. Each meeting will be an hour
and a half.
