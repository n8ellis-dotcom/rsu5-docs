Finance Subcommittee Report
Date: June 22, 2017
Committee: Finance Committee
Chair: John Morang
In attendance: John Morang, Kate Brown, Michelle Lickteig and Dr. Becky Foley
Guests: Dennis Ouellette
Meeting Date: June 14, 2017
Agenda Items and Discussion:
Durham Land:
Dennis Ouellette presented to the committee a letter from Matt Cartmell of Keller Williams Realty,
stating the property was very small and would likely not be usable for the school district. The
committee agreed on an amount they were comfortable with for the purchase price and asked
Dennis to approach the interested party.
Busing:
It was presented to the committee that BOMAR transportation has approached Dennis stating they
will not be able to carry out the contract for Durham busing for FY18. Dennis explored options
with BOMAR and Cyr Transportation in trying to lease buses from them. Michelle L. presented
the committee with the expenses related to those two options and the option of bringing everything
in house. It was agreed that in the long run, money would be saved by bringing that route in house,
as well as give consistency by having all transportation operating in the same manner throughout
the district.
Next Meeting:
Fall of 2017, schedule to come.
