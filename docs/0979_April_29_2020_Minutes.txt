Finance Subcommittee Report
Date: May 11, 2020
Committee: Finance
Chair: Kate Brown
In attendance: Kate Brown, Michelle Ritcheson, Jeremy Clough, Becky Foley, Scott Vaitones
Meeting Date: April 29, 2020
Spring stipends were discussed. Athletic Director Craig Sickels recommended paying the varsity
coaches for a third of their stipend to reimburse them for work done in preparation of the spring
season. Michelle Ritcheson made a motion, with a second from Jeremy Clough to pay the varsity
spring coaches a third of their stipend for work completed in preparation of the spring season. Vote
was unanimous.
The committee also heard from Director of Operations Dennis Ouellette. He discussed the audit
proposals he received from two companies: Trane and Mechanical Services. He recommended going
with Mechanical Services. There was a motion by Michelle Ritcheson to enter into a contract with
Mechanical Services for a comprehensive energy audit. This was seconded by Jeremy Clough. The
vote was unanimous. Dennis will reach out to Mechanical Services to schedule the audit.
Lastly, the interim business manager Scott Vaitones reviewed the warrant articles to ensure the amount
of the adopted budget aligned with the amount in the articles. There were a couple of areas that were
questioned, but were cleared up in the next 24 hours. All agree that as written the amounts in the
warrant currently balance with the Board adopted budget.
