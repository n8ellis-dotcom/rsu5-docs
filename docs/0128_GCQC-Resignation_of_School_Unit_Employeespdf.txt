NEPN/NSBA Code: GCQC
RESIGNATION OF SCHOOL UNIT EMPLOYEES
The RSU No. 5 Board of Directors authorizes the Superintendent to accept all employee
resignations. Such acceptance shall be effective immediately upon acceptance by the
Superintendent. Acceptance shall be confirmed in writing to the employee. The
resignation and its acceptance should be reported as information to the Board at the next
regular or special meeting.
