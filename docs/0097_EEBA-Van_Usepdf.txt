NEPN/NSBA Code: EEBA
VAN USE
The van is available for activities to enrich the education of the students of RSU No. 5 and
programs of Community Programs.
Any driver of the van must be a district employee or approved volunteer, be 21 years of age or
older and licensed by the State of Maine.
The Superintendent shall be responsible for developing and administering appropriate procedures
to implement this policy.
Adopted: April 28, 2010
