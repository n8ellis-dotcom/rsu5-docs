Susan Dee shared a presentation of the timeline and overview of our Comprehensive Literacy
Framework in PK-5 since the fall of 2017 through present day. The presentation outlined our
revisions to the curriculum overtime and the importance of the role of coaching and ongoing
professional development in RSU5. The presentation culminated with a review of the current
reading and writing units including phonics, grammar and spelling.
