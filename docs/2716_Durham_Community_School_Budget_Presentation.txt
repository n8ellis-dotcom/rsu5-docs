FY 26 BUDGET: SCHOOL BOARD BUDGET WORKSHOP PRESENTATION
Building/Department: Durham Community School
What key issues and priorities are you trying to address in your proposed budget?
Our proposed budget reflects an intent to reduce expenditures while maintaining
programming and the educational experience for students. Given the projected
enrollment numbers, the current 2nd grade cohort will shift from being placed across
three homerooms to being placed across two homerooms in grade 3 next year.
Likewise, the current 4th grade cohort will shift from being placed across three
homerooms to being placed across two homerooms in grade 5 next year.
As we look to reduce where possible, we also are aware of the need to add supports
where and when needed. We are requesting the addition of an Ed Tech III to support
our Multi-Tiered System of Support (MTSS) for Response to Intervention-Behavior (RTI-
B) in grades 5-8. We have a robust program in grades K-4 but nothing at all for grades
5-8. This has created an issue for some students who were successful with the support
in lower grades then losing some of those gains when the support is removed.
