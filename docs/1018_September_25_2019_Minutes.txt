Agenda Items and Discussion:
Next Steps on Cost Sharing:
The Committee continued to work on the presentation slides of the cost sharing formula. A little more
research on other RSUs was done to make sure the committee can explain with more detail during the
presentation. Kate will be emailing Town and Council Leadership to invite them to the upcoming School
Board meetings in reference to the cost sharing formula. Michelle L showed her compilation of handouts for
the next School Board meeting. Some revisions were requested. Michelle R will be putting together a
handout of a comparison of the current model with the proposed model with the committees recommended
formula and the 100-0 and 0-100 (valuation-student count) comparisons
The Committee will be doing an overview of the current cost sharing formula with the School Board on
October 9. Then the presentation of the proposed recommendation from the Committee will be given to the
School Board on October 23 with a vote on the recommendation to happen at the November 6 School Board
meeting.
