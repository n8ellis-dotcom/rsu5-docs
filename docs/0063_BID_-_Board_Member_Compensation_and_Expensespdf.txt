NEPN/NSBA Code: BID
BOARD MEMBER COMPENSATION AND EXPENSES
A stipend of $25.00 per meeting for each Board member is authorized. This payment will be
made for attendance at all regular meetings and for attendance at special Board of Director
meetings when minutes are prepared and subsequently accepted.
