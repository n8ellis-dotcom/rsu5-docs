Finance Subcommittee Report
Date: October 17, 2012
Committee: Finance Subcommittee
Chair: John Morang
Meeting Date: October 10, 2012
Members Present: John Morang, Kate Brown, John Ricker, and Shannon Welsh
Agenda Items and Discussion:
Warrant Signing:
Transfer warrant dated October 10, 2012 was signed. Finance Director Kelly Wentworth
explained that the District Music Leadership Team met and reported back to her on how to
distribute the $9,100 of music money that was appropriated in Contingency in the 2012/2013
budget cycle. Kelly also reported that the Leadership Team discussed how to spend the Janet
Newlin donation funds received in September 2010. These monies are currently held in the
Donations account and will be expended throughout this school year to purchase equipment,
which will enhance each school’s music program.
Bonding Representative:
Finance Director reported that she had spoken with Joseph Cuetara from Moors & Cabot. Mr.
Cuetara is the Representative that recently worked on the Town of Freeport’s bonding refunding.
He has also worked with several neighboring districts. He is willing to come speak to the
Finance Committee regarding the bonding process, various financing options, and current rates.
He will join us at our November 14, 2012 meeting.
Capital Projects/Efficiencies Presentation:
Transportation and Facilities Director Dennis Ouellette reviewed the updated Capital Plan that he
would be presenting later that evening to the full Board of Directors. Dennis also took time to
review the capital items that have been completed in the 2012/2013 school year as well as
reported on efficiencies created by completed projects.
Technology Presentation:
Technology Director Seth Thompson presented his updated draft of the Techology plan that he
will be presenting to the full Board at the November 14, 2012 meeting. MLTI renewal is in
2013/2014 and the budget reflects new laptops for Teachers as well as students grades 6-12.
Warrant Signing:
Payroll Warrant signed.
Next Meeting: November 14, 2012
