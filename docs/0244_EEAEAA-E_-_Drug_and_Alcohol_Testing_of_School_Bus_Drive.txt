DRUG AND ALCOHOL TESTING OF SCHOOL BUS DRIVERS
FEDERAL MOTOR CARRIER SAFETY ADMINISTRATION CONSENT FORM
The Federal Motor Carrier Safety Administration (FMCSA) has created a new database
to review the drug and alcohol testing policies. All bus drivers with a Commercial
Driver’s License (CDL) are required to register with the FMCSA by 01/06/2020 and
annually thereafter. This is to determine if the school unit employees have ever had a
drug or alcohol program violation which would impact the safety functions of operating
commercial motor vehicles.
Please sign and date this consent form to register your name on the Clearinghouse.
