Finance Committee Minutes

Committee Members Absent:
Administrators: Superintendent Tom Gray, Director of Finance & HR Kelly Wentworth
Minutes
Meeting called to order at 5:19 P.M.
1) ECSE Transition & Future Planning Considerations
a. Early Childhood Special Education (ECSE) Assistant Director
i. This position will implement the Early Childhood Programming that the
district will begin in FY27 for four year old students with IEPs and in FY28
for three year old students with IEPs. This is to comply with the state
mandate to program for them beginning July 1, 2028. Director Wentworth
explained that the funding for this position for the remainder of the FY26
school year could come from the 5.5 Special Education Ed Tech vacancies
that have not been filled.
b. Facility Study
i. In response to a confluence of factors coming up within the next few years,
Superintendent Gray is requesting funding to conduct a Districtwide
Facilities Study of all buildings. The recommendation is to fund up to
$100,000 from Capital Project Reserves.
2) Cost Sharing Review
a. Director Wentworth presented the updated version of the cost sharing calculations
spreadsheet for review and consideration. The Committee members felt that the
percentages remain consistent to the previous year amounts which were reviewed
by the full Board of Directors; therefore no recommendations for changes were
made.
3) Insurance Bidding Update

4) FY27 Proposed Budget Review
a. Superintendent Gray reviewed the FY28 & FY29 proposed budget projections that
Director Wentworth completed as a result of a request from the Board of Directors.
The proposals included two options, one that included the initial FY27 budget
before any reductions and one that includes the FY27 Superintendent’s
Recommended Budget which include Tier 1 & 2 reductions. This information would
be shared with the fill Board at their meeting later that evening.
