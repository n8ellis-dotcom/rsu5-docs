Finance Subcommittee Report
Date: April 20, 2017
Committee: Finance Committee
Chair: John Morang
In attendance: John Morang, Kate Brown, Michelle Ritcheson (by phone), Michelle Lickteig and
Dr. Becky Foley
Guests:
Meeting Date: April 12, 2017
Agenda Items and Discussion:
Review of Final 2017/2018 Budget/Impact:
Michelle L. reviewed with the committee the final numbers since the approval by the school board
of the Board of Directors Adopted Budget, showing a 3.96% expenditure increase and an overall
district tax impact of 5.25%. Michelle L. also reviewed the estimated individual Town tax impact
of Durham at 3.05%, Freeport at 3.50% and Pownal at 5.82%. Michelle L. reminded the
committee, these are still estimates, as she does not have the current year town valuations and mil
rates yet.
Review of warrant language regarding receipt of additional State Subsidy:
Michelle L. read the four options to the committee for use of additional State Subsidy usage if
received after budget approval process. The committee discussed. Decision was to be made by the
board that night.
Our current standing with Grant Funding reimbursements:
After notification from the auditor of being two years in arrears for reimbursements. Michelle L.
reported that those two years are caught up to date. She is working on the current year and hopes to
have the current year caught up to date by the time the next Finance Committee meeting.
Other:
Two days prior to the meeting, it was discovered that there was a technical defect in the posting of
the Bond Referendum Town meeting and, therefore, bond counsel is unable to give an unqualified
opinion. Different options of how to correct this have been explored and those options were
discussed with the finance committee.
Next Meeting:
May 10, 2017 at Durham Community School (although the schedule indicates Central Office, the
board meeting that night is at DCS)
