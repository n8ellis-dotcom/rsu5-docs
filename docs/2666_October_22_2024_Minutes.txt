Curriculum and Program Development Committee Report
Committee: Curriculum and Program Development
Meeting date: October 22, 2024
Committee Members in attendance: Danielle George, Maura Pillsbury, Carolyn Jensen
Administrators: Holly Johnson, Will PIdden, Cynthia Alexander
Guests: none
An overview of the year-long curriculum work in science at the 3-6 level was presented to the
committee. The presentation outlined our plan for developing and implementing science units
connected to phenomena-based learning, and alignment to the Next Generation Science
Standards.
The outcomes this year have focused on collective learning and growth through professional
development, implementing phenomena-based instruction, and focusing on the cross-cutting
standards and science practices.
