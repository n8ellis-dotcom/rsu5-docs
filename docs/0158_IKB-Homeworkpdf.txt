NEPN/NSBA Code: IKB
HOMEWORK
The RSU No. 5 Board believes that homework should be meaningful, engaging, process-
oriented, and integrative, while being mindful of the diverse capacities of students.
Homework helps students apply the skills they have been taught in school. As a child
advances through school, it is reasonable that the amount of homework can be expected
to increase.
Schools will communicate homework expectations and supports to students and
parents/guardians.
