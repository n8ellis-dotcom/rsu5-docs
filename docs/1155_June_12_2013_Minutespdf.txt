Finance Subcommittee Report

Chair: John Morang
Meeting Date: June 12, 2013
Members Present: Kate Brown, John Ricker, and Shannon Welsh
Excused: John Morang
Agenda Items and Discussion:
Review of Final 2013/2014 Warrant & Meeting Schedule:
Finance Director presented the final versions of the 2013/2014 Finance Meeting Schedule and
Warrant Schedule. Meeting Schedule and Warrant will be distributed/posted so that it is
available to the public.
Review of Final 2013/2014 Tax Impact:
Finance Director presented/reviewed the final version of the RSU5 Budget 2013-2014 Impact
document, which supports the individual Town Assessment Warrants that the Board of
Director’s would be signing at tonight’s Board Meeting. The overall budget increase was 3.8%.
Impact to individual communities as compared to previous year: Durham 10.05%; Freeport
3.12%, and Pownal 8.42%.
MaineCare Seed Update:
Finance Director reported the total cost of MaineCare Seed for 2012/2013 was $213,891.50. It
was possible that a transfer warrant would be necessary in order to cover the expenditures within
the Special Education article. Student needs, staff absences and illness, as well as MaineCare
Seed costs had really put a strain on the budgeted funds. The Finance Director would notify the
Finance Committee if that was needed and a meeting would be held on June 28 or 30th.
Warrant Signing:
Warrants signed.
Next Meeting: (Possible) June 28 or 30, 2013
