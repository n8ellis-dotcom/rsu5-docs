Agenda Items and Discussion:
Budget Review:
Finance Director reviewed the revised Budget Impact sheet which reflects the additions made at
the May 1, 2013 Board Meeting. Kelly explained that the Department of Education had made
revisions to some districts ED279 reports, which is the EPS document the state creates to provide
our state subsidy information. The most recent ED279 decreased our state subsidy by $154,601
from the initial estimate and reported the expected full value impact of State Retirement to be
$268,750.67, the budget impact document had been updated to reflect these changes.
Review of Draft 2013/2014 Warrant & Meeting Schedule:
Finance Director presented the draft versions of the 2013/2014 Finance Meeting Schedule and
Warrant Schedule. Meeting schedule is subject to change based on the Board of Director’s
adopted schedule. The Finance Committee agreed to meet on the evening of the first monthly
Board meeting. Updated schedules will be presented for review at the June 12, 2013 meeting.
