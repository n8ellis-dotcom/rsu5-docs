Review RPC Funding Formula:
The Finance Committee reviewed the cost sharing section of the RSU5 Reorganization Plan. Kelly
reviewed definitions of terms and explained how they tied into the project tax impact sheet that is
distributed during the budget process.
The budget is calculated along the following guide:
Total RSU Spending Budget
Less State and Local Non Shared Debt
Less Local Shared Revenues and State Aid to Education
Less RSU Plan Required Local Contribution
Equal Additional Local Monies Required
Additional Local Monies Required are then multiplied by the Cost Sharing percentage (Durham
21.42%; Freeport 65.98%, Pownal 12.60%) to determine each Town’s Additional Local Contribution.
The Additional Local Contribution is then added to the RSU Plan Required Local Contribution to
determine each Town’s annual assessment.
Old Business:
The Finance Director recommended and gained Finance Committee approval to delay the banking, audit,
and insurance request for proposal process until after the Town of Freeport vote in withdrawal. These
request for proposals cover a time period of 3-5 years and it was felt that the information contained in the rfp
could change drastically over the next year and certainly for 3-5 year period included in the proposals.
