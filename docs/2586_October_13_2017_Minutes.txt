FHS Track and Field Design Committee
Friday, October 13, 2017
Freeport High School
Minutes
Attendance: John Simoneau, Andrew Johnston, Tim Reich, Brad Williams, Dennis Ouellette, John
Paterson, Becky Foley, Kim LaMarre, Craig Sickels, Fred Palmer, Michelle Ritcheson, Charlie
Mellon, Michelle Lickteig.
Updates:
1. Timeline update:
a. Turf crew should be completed next week.
b. 90% on drainage on the site. Currently working on shaping the south side of the
field, preparing for the donor area.
c. Working on path coming from the Gap parking lot.
d. They’re to subgrade on the softball field.
e. LLBean pole has been moved.
f. Track surface – we have an issue with temperature going forward. There is an
option that we are recommending which includes putting down a base layer
with the Nike Grind. This can then be striped for competitions and they can wait
to lay the topcoat. This allows for the facility to be used this spring. This will be
at no additional cost to the RSU to line the track.
g. June 18-July 13th the topcoat will be installed and the project completed.
h. Most of granite for the donor area has been delivered.
i. We’ve kept 5% retainage in all lines.
2. Budget
a. $124,000 remaining; does not include sod for the softball field.
b. Working on budget for Pat, Brooke, Andrew for the additional costs.
3. Art Update
a. Arch area donors are working with Pat on the design.
b. FALCON PRIDE was the slogan chosen by the students to place on the wall.
c. Communicating with Nike around this to schedule when it will happen.
4. Other:
a. School Board agreed to make the bleachers the priority.
i. All in system would cost $207,000 but doesn’t include concrete pad.
ii. $143,000 is an angle frame bleacher system with press box. Dant Clayton
is a nicer system.
iii. Looking for $175,000 project for the bleachers.
iv. Committee decided to bid out an angle frame bleachers at 500 seats, with
add alt to go to 750, separate add alt for press box, include a filming
platform. Get RFP out in January. System would be ready for fall sports.
v. Concrete pad is needed – at an approximate cost of $47,300.
b. Capacity for seating – Craig shared area districts’ attendance at various sporting
events.
Next meeting: Nov. 3rd
