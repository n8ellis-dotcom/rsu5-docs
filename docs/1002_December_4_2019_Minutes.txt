Agenda Items and Discussion:
Cost sharing options based on Board feedback on November 20, 2019:
The Committee considered what additional analysis they could prepare to present to the School Board to
help them determine which cost sharing model will create the fairest result. They decided to run historical
data to the degree possible through a number of models for comparison’s sake, as well as to look in-depth at
the cost sharing model used by RSU1.
