FY 26 BUDGET: SCHOOL BOARD BUDGET WORKSHOP PRESENTATION
Building/Department: School Nutrition Department
What key issues and priorities are you addressing in your proposed budget?
RSU 5’s Nutrition Program supports student learning and wellness through a variety of
school meal offerings that satisfy students' nutritional needs. FY26’s budget addresses
the district’s high student meal participation rate and the associated costs. The Nutrition
Department strives to ensure student meal access despite changes to federal
programming or reimbursement.
