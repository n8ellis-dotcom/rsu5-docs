Finance Committee Minutes
Wednesday, March 26, 2025
5:00 p.m. - Freeport High School
Committee Members Present: Kelly Sink, Beth Munsen, Michelle Ritcheson
Committee Members Absent: None
Administrators: Jean Skorapa, Kelly Wentworth
Minutes
1. Meeting called to order at 5:05 p.m.
2. Cost Sharing Review -
a. Director Wentworth presented the updated version of the cost sharing calculations
spreadsheet for review and consideration. The Committee members felt that the
percentages remain consistent to the previous years amounts which were reviewed by
the full Board of Directors; therefore no recommendations for changes were made.
3. FY24 Audit Update -
a. The Finance Director reported that Marge, the Auditor, had sent the draft for review.
Director Wentworth was in the process of reviewing the draft and would sign off once
completed. The Auditor will present and review highlights of the audit at our April
Finance meeting.
4. Other -
a. The Finance Committee discussed the recent public comment made by Mr. Fruend in
regards to alternative health insurance plans that may offer the district savings. They
asked Director Wentwoth to seek information from other districts to see if they had
explored alternate health insurance offerings and whether or not they had made any
recommendations for change.
b. Next meeting of the Finance Committee, April 30, 2025 @ PES.
Meeting adjourned at 6:03 P.M.
