Regional School Unit 5
Durham • Freeport • Pownal
“To inspire and support every learner by challenging minds, building character, sparking creativity, and nurturing passions.”
Becky Foley, Ph.D., Superintendent of Schools Cynthia Alexander, Assistant Superintendent of Schools
Rick Kusturin, Director of Finance & Human Resources Bonnie Violette, Ph.D., Director of Instructional Support
Date: January 7, 2021
Committee: Finance
Chair: Jeremy Clough
In attendance: Michelle Ritcheson, Jeremy Clough, Beth Munsen, Rick Kusturin.
Absent: none
Meeting Date: December 9th, 2020, Freeport High School cafeteria.
The Chair of the Finance Committee opened the meeting at 5:01 pm.
The Committee was then briefed on the current status of the FY21 Budget. No significant issues were
reported upon.
The Committee was briefed on the progress of the FY22 budget work. No significant issues were
discussed.
The Committee was requested to consider and approve the movement of the Food Service Director from
a social security benefits position to the Maine Public Employee Retirement System (MPERS). Motion
to approve was moved by the Chair and seconded by Michelle Ritcheson. The motion carried
unanimously.
The meeting was adjourned at 5:47 pm. The next scheduled meeting of the Finance Committee is
January 13th at 5:00 pm at the Freeport High School cafeteria.
