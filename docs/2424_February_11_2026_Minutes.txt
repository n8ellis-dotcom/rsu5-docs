Minutes
Committee Members Present: Michelle Ritcheson, Kelly Sink, Karen Breer
Committee Members Absent: None
Administrators in Attendance: Tom Gray, Superintendent; Glen Reynolds, Director of Facilities; Kelly
Wentworth, Director of Finance and Human Resources
Guest: Molly Meyer, FHS Tennis Coach
Agenda Items and Discussion:
Meeting called to order at 5:00pm
1. FHS Tennis courts update- Director Reynolds updated the committee that the contract
for design services was signed and CHA has scheduled the site survey to be completed on
2/18/26. Director Reynolds indicated that the core sampling has not yet been scheduled.
2. Energy Audit Phase 1 project update- Director Reynolds updated the committee that the
lighting project has been completed at Pownal Elementary and the District Office.
Director Rerynolds shared that the lighting vendor is currently working at Freepoort
Middle School. The next location for this project is Mast Landing School.
3. DCS automation replacement- Director Reynolds updated the committee that the
contract has been signed and material is being ordered. EMC is the vendor who bid the
project, and Maine Controls was the winning bidder.
4. FHS baseball dugout roof replacement donated by Boosters- Director Reynolds updated
the committee that the baseball boosters have asked if they can replace the two roofs on
the baseball dugouts at Freeport High School. The estimated cost for this replacement is
around $3,000.00 dollars. Director Reyolds shared that new roofs will be the same as the
current ones on the softball dugouts as they are metal. The boosters would like to begin
the replacement in mid March before baseball season.

5. Guest: Molly Meyer FHS Tennis Coach shared some information regarding the applying
for a USTA grant to help offset the cost of installing new tennis courts. Molly provided the
committee with a document showing what information would be needed for approval of
the grant. Molly also shared that the grant for indoor courts would be estimated at
$35,000 per court and $25,000 per court for outdoor courts. The maximum grant
amount awarded is $150,000.
Motion to adjourn was made by Michelle Ritcheson second by Kelly Sink at 5:18 p.m.
