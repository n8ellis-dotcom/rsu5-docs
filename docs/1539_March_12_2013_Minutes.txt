Strategic Communications Subcommittee Report

Committee: Strategic Communications
Chair: Jane Blais
Meeting Dates: March 12, 2013
Agenda Items and Discussion:
State of the Schools draft report
Joe Makley presented final drafts of the data gathered to assess the RSU5 Strategic Plan, including
NECAP and MHSA scores, Financial data, College attendance, etc. The new report will include
summaries of the action taken in response to the implications presented by the data. The 2013 State of
the Schools report will be mailed to every household during the last week of March.
Dine and Discuss sessions:
The committee discussed formats for the three “Dine and Discuss” sessions, one in each of the towns.
Sessions will each last one hour, and have a round table format. Topics will include the State of the
Schools report, the 2013-2014 Proposed Budget and the Freeport High School Renovation Project.
Conversations will take place from 5:30-6:30 p.m. on the following dates:
Wednesday, April 10, 2013 – Pownal Elementary School
Wednesday, April 24, 2013 – Durham Community School
Wednesday, May 1, 2013 – Freeport High School
Next Strategic Communications Meeting: May 30, 2013 @ 4:30 p.m. at the Central Office
