Strategic Communications Subcommittee Report
Committee: Strategic Communications

Valy Steverlynck
Also in attendance: Naomi Ledbetter
Agenda Items and Discussion:
Work on surveys for Unity and Pride Project
The updated Communication Suggestions version was shared and finalized. Minor revisions were
made. This will be distributed to the administrative team next week, with Becky highlighting the ones
she feels is most critical.
Satisfaction Survey: This was reviewed by the B Team in September, and is now ready to be
formatted. The committee decided that it would be given some time in the spring
Exit Survey: Feedback was gathered from the B Team meeting in September. Administrators believe
that it needs to be rewritten with a more neutral tone, so that feedback is solicited whether it is positive
or negative from parents. Becky will present a rewritten draft at the next Strategic Communications’
Meeting.
Unity & Pride Survey: The committee revised the Unity & Pride Survey. The draft will be presented to
the Board on September 28th for feedback.
Cost Estimates for Renaming: Becky shared some preliminary data on costs that would be incurred if
the high school were renamed. Becky solicited feedback from the Board members on how much detail
and materials are needed when presenting to the Board on this subject.
Next meeting will focus on finalizing the Unity & Pride Survey and outlining a process to be utilized
for decision making around whether to rename the high school or not.
