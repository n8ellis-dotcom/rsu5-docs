FY 26 BUDGET: SCHOOL BOARD BUDGET WORKSHOP PRESENTATION
Building/Department: Mast Landing School
What key issues and priorities are you trying to address in your proposed budget?
Our proposed budget reflects an intent to reduce expenditures while maintaining
programming and the educational experience for students. Given the projected
enrollment numbers, the current 3rd grade cohort will shift from being placed across five
homerooms to being placed across four homerooms in grade 4 next year. This shift
eliminated the need to fund the 1-year-only position this year that was funded from the
reserve account.
Additionally, the proposed budget reduces staffing by one regular education educational
technician. This position was created previously to serve as a bench sub and–when not
pulled to cover staff vacancies–to provide academic support in collaboration with our
literacy and math interventionists. Given that we have observed a notable increase in
the number and quality of our substitute teachers in the district, the need for this
position has lessened.
