Agreed upon Norms
● Trust collective wisdom and give some time and thought to ideas before passing
judgment.
● Be conscious of your own and others’ assumptions, and always go to the source to seek
accurate information.
● Assume good intentions, especially during the busiest and most stressful times. Intentions
do not negate impact.
● Maintain confidentiality concerning students, educators, and families with whom we work.
● Express a Growth Mindset. We have to support our learning by accepting and encouraging
each other to learn from mistakes as well as from success. Do not try and avoid the
discomfort around difficult conversations.
● Seek out and center the participation or experiences of those most impacted by the
topic at hand.
Adopted 11/14/23
DEI[B] Committee Charge: The DEI[B] Committee is charged with creating a culture in RSU
No. 5 where diversity, equity, and inclusion are respected and celebrated through the
implementation of thoughtful, iterative, and innovative strategies that inspire an inclusive
mindset in RSU No. 5 employees, students, and community members.

To better execute this charge, the Committee had adopted the following definitions (Guiding
Definitions HERE) of the terms core to our work. These definitions have been adapted from
resources developed by a range of secondary and higher education institutions who have
researched these domains.
Agenda Items and Discussion:
1. Share Introductions - Committee members and guests introduced themselves
2. 10 minutes Jigsaw Guiding Definitions - How can we explain these guiding terms more
succinctly?
Diversity - Awareness of all the different types of diverse humans in the world
Equity - Recognizing & Responding to individual needs so that every person has access to
opportunities and support required to succeed.
Inclusion - Embracing differences and eliminating barriers
Belonging - Support and Connection - Feeling included by showing your true authentic self
and experiencing support and connection.
