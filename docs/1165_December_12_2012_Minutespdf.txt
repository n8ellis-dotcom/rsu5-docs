Review of RPC Funding Formula:
The Finance Committee reviewed the cost sharing section of the RSU5 Reorganization Plan.
Kelly reviewed definitions of terms and explained how they tied into the project tax impact sheet
that is distributed during the budget process.
The budget is calculated along the following guide:
Total RSU Spending Budget
Less State and Local Non Shared Debt
Less Local Shared Revenues and State Aid to Education
Less RSU Plan Required Local Contribution
Equal Additional Local Monies Required

Additional Local Monies Required are then multiplied by the Cost Sharing percentage (Durham
21.42%; Freeport 65.98%, Pownal 12.60%) to determine each Town’s Additional Local
Contribution. The Additional Local Contribution is then added to the RSU Plan Required Local
Contribution to determine each Town’s annual assessment.
