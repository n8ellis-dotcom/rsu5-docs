Meeting Date: November 18, 2018
Agenda Items and Discussion:
Next Steps on Cost Sharing:
The Committee reviewed each town’s letter to the School Board and their individual
recommendations. The Committee also reviewed the FAQ that the RPC Committee put together to
share the process for and decision for the current cost sharing formula. Discussion on how the
numbers from the ED 279 (state subsidy calculation) and the calculation of the cost sharing formula
took place so that each committee member understood the calculation as is it today. To help with
that, Michelle L. shared the calculation spreadsheet with the committee for them to see the actual
formulas for the calculation. Michelle L. was tasked with asking the DOE a question for follow up
clarification. Michelle L. was also asked to pull out the binder on the minutes from the RPC
meetings to review in the next Finance Committee meeting. Michelle R. and Kate were going to do
some research of their own trying to get to why this particular formula was decided on at the time
of the RSU formation out of the seven that were discussed. The Committee agreed the next
meeting would be to go over the data and information we gathered.
