Committee Members Absent:
Administrators in Attendance: Tom Gray, Superintendent; Glen Reynolds, Director of Facilities;
Kelly Wentworth, Director of Finance and Human Resources
Agenda Items and Discussion:
Meeting called to order at 5:21 p.m.
1. Update on Board Facilities Tour. - Director Reynolds updated the Committee that the tour is
scheduled for December 12, 2025 starting at 9am. The tour will start at Freeport High.
Committee member Kelly Sink asked if there is a theme to this year's tour. Director Reynolds
mentioned it will be over the phase one projects that are in process or completed. Director
Reynolds also mentioned the newly installed leach field system at Pownal Elementary as part
of the tour.
2. Update on the Capital Improvement Plan for 2026/2027-2027/2028- Director Reynolds shared
the Capital Improvement plan with the committee. Director Reynolds mentioned the 24/25 roof
replacement projects for the high school will be grouped with the proposed 25/26 roof
replacements listed in the plan in order to obtain the best pricing. Director of Finance Kelly
Wentworth mentioned that the 24/25 roof replacements would be paid for out of the Capital
Reserve funds and not out of the Capital Projects funds that are part of the local budget.
Motion to adjourn was made by Michelle Ritcheson second by Kelly Sink at 5:46 p.m.
