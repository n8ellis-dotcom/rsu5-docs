Agenda Items and Discussion:
1. Budget Brochure - The committee reviewed the budget brochure and determined that the
format worked and no revisions were needed.
2. Marketing Brochure - The committee decided to distribute the brochure to each of the
following locations: all six schools, town libraries, and town halls. They will also be available
at Board meetings
3. Satisfaction Survey – The committee determined to do this survey once every three years.
4. Student Exit Survey - The data was distributed for the past year. Committee members will read
the results before the next meeting. At this meeting it will be decided how to summarize the
results. The committee discussed reviewing the data twice yearly: in June in preparation for the
Board retreat, and in November in preparation for budget requests.
