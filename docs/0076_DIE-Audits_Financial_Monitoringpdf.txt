NEPN/NSBA Code: DIE
AUDITS/FINANCIAL MONITORING
The school system's books shall be audited annually by the Maine Department of Auditors or by
an outside auditor approved by the Board, based on the July 1 to June 30 fiscal year. The
Superintendent/designee shall be responsible for working with auditors in their conduct of
annual or other audits as required.
