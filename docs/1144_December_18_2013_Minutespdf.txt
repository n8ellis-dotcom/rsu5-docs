Agenda Items and Discussion:
Review of RFP for Banking, Audit, & Insurance:
Director of Finance explained the process for competitive bidding for property and casualty, school
board legal liability, boiler and auto insurance coverages. Competitive bidding for insurance services is
required by law every five years and would need to be completed this spring for the 2014/2015 school
year. Director of Finance had emailed DOE to see if a waiver could be granted with the withdrawal
process pending. Banking and Audit services could also be considered at this same time although not
required by law.
