Finance Subcommittee Report
April 11, 2013
Committee: Finance Subcommittee
Chair: John Morang
Meeting Date: April 10, 2013
Members Present: John Morang, Kate Brown, John Ricker, and Shannon Welsh
Agenda Items and Discussion:
Warrant Signing:
Payroll warrants signed.
Budget 2013/2014 Update:
Finance Director reported that there were no adjustments to be made at this time. Kelly and
Superintendent Welsh continue to monitor what is happening with the Legislature and Department of
Education and will keep the Finance Committee and Board informed as we work through the process.
There was a discussion regarding in district travel expenses for staff shared between buildings as well
as the reduction of a district level Strategist position.
Transfer Warrant #3:
The Finance Director presented transfer warrant #3 to which moves $50,000 from the Contingency
account in Article 6 to the Professional Services account of the High School also within Article 6 to
cover the cost of FHS Renovation Concept Design work as well as the advertising and promotion of
the referendum. Finance Committee approved and signed the transfer warrant.
2011/2012 Audit Review:
The Finance Director presented and reviewed the bound version of the 2011/2012 financial audit.
There were no material weaknesses or significant deficiencies that were identified. There were
suggestions for improvements. Kelly then reviewed the plan that is being implemented to address the
recommendations of the Auditor.
