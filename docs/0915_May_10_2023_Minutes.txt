Finance Committee Minutes
May 10, 2023
Freeport High School Cafeteria
In Attendance:
Beth Munsen, Chair
Michelle Ritcheson
Maura Pillsbury
Jean Skorapa
Peggy Brown
Chair Munsen called the meeting to order at 5:17 p.m.
FY23 Financial Update:
Peggy reviewed the April financial report and indicated that 83.33% of the fiscal year has passed and 81.5% of revenues
have been received and posted. The April interest revenue was $30,499.30 but had not yet been posted as the report was
prepared on the morning of May 1st. Interest rates ranged from 4.79% to 4.83% during the month of April. Expenditures for
April were 71.31%.
Peggy referenced Article 10. The bond payment exceeds this line by $1.54 which would result in it going negative, needing
a Finance Committee vote decision to move $1.54 from Article 1 to cover. Peggy discussed options she had previously
discussed with Jean. Michelle requested Peggy seek guidance from our auditor.
FY22 Audit Update:
Peggy reached out to our auditor, Marge Hall, prior to this meeting. Her office is wrapping up the review of her report and
she hopes to present us with the draft report next week. She is hopeful it will be all wrapped up by the audit extension
deadline of May 31st.
FY24 Budget Update:
Peggy let the committee know she has checked in with the three towns to confirm their presence at the May 24th Annual
Budget meeting at Durham Community School. The towns will need to be present to check in the voters. Peggy will also
confirm with our attorney, Greg Im.
Warrant Schedule:
Peggy reviewed the draft with the committee and suggested a few edits. The committee was fine with those edits.
17 West Street Freeport, ME 04032 · 207-865-0928

Other:
The committee members used the rest of the meeting to discuss cost sharing. At the board meeting later on this date, the
board will have the opportunity to discuss the recent presentation by Suzan Beaudoin and contemplate whether or not they
would like to adjust the cost sharing formula. Michelle felt it would be beneficial at a later date to have the board vote to
continue with the current formula or to change. A formal decision would provide closure and confirm it was reviewed on a
periodic basis.
Adjournment:
Chair Munsen requested a motion to adjourn at 5:57 p.m. The motion was provided by Maura Pillsbury and was seconded
by Beth Munsen. Vote was unanimous (Munsen, Ritcheson, Pillsbury).
Respectfully submitted,
Peggy Brown
Interim Director of Finance & HR
