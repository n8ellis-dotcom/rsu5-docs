Strategic Communications Subcommittee Report
Committee: Strategic Communications
Meeting date: June 9, 2017
Chair: Candy deCsipkes
Committee Members in attendance: Kate Brown, Candy deCsipkes, Valy Steverlynck,
Lindsay Sterling, Becky Foley
Began discussion about creating a brochure to market the RSU. Brochures from
other districts were reviewed and members discussed the pros/cons of each format.
The committee decided on a tri-fold brochure that would focus on multiple
categories. Following committee members will create a draft page to bring to the
September meeting to review and revise:
Page 1: Our Schools: Description of each of the 6 schools – Kate Brown
Page 2: Front Page: Candy deCsipkes
Page 3: Celebrations/Awards: Lindsay Sterling
Page 4: Offerings/Curriculum: Becky Foley
Page 5: Atmosphere: Quotes from teachers, parents, students – Valy Steverlynck
