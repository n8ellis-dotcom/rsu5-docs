NEPN/NSBA Code: JLCDAA-R
MEDICAL ALERT INFORMATION PROCEDURE
Each school year, school nurses will create a process with administrators for
disseminating Medical Alert Information to school staff who have a legitimate ‘need to
know’ in compliance with FERPA Law. The information will be compiled from the
Student Physical and Athletic Exam Form, and/or the annual Student Health History
Form which includes a parent permission signature for release of information. The school
nurse will enter the information into the Emergency Medical page in PowerSchool. All
staff should check for a medical alert icon in PowerSchool for all students on their roster.
The appropriate information on students with potentially life threatening medical
emergencies (e.g. asthma, seizures, diabetes, cardiac conditions, bee sting and food
allergies, et. al.) will be shared with the appropriate administrators and reviewed with
personnel who have direct contact with the student (such as teachers, food services staff,
bus drivers,). Any medical alert information is to be kept strictly confidential and will be
updated by the school nurse as needed.
