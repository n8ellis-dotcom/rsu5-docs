1. RFP Update
a. Pownal Elementary School Septic
1. The district received six proposals.
1. CCC Construction LLC - $179,755
2. Copp Excavating, Inc - $213, 904
3. R.N. Willey & Sons Excavating - $163,709.86
4. Steadfast Earthworks - $221,800
5. A+ Septic, Inc. - $284,981
6. Bursey Enterprises - $162,682
2. The following criteria was used to select the proposal deemed to provide
the best value for RSU5:
1. Proposer Experience and Customer Service History - 50%
2. Availability for installations and completion date deadline - 40%
3. Durability/Warranty & Ease of Maintenance - 10%
3. Copp Excavating, Inc. was selected for the project.
