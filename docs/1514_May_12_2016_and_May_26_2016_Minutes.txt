May 12, 2016 Meeting
Present: Kate Brown, Candy deCsipkes, Lindsay Sterling, Valy Steverlynk and Mike Lafortune
At this meeting the group discussed/brainstormed about an exit survey and a "branding/marketing"
plan. They talked briefly about a general satisfaction survey.
"Branding/Marketing":
A suggested name for this effort could be "Unity and Pride Project" (UPP). A possible goal for the
effort might be: to create pride, unity, engagement, excitement and a sense of belonging in our
school district. Possible strategy might be: to have students, staff, parents, and community
members explore the district's identity; including who we are, what we're great at, what we're known
for and what we'd like to be known for. The process could include having a staff member assigned
the task of overseeing this effort; having students explore the history of the towns in the district and
visit schools; having each school explore their identity; holding community meetings; and looking at
logos, names, etc. -- visual identity. The group visited the renaming of the high school. THIS IS
ALL VERY PRELIMINARY AND STILL UNDER DISCUSSION.
Much of this meeting was a visit from Nancy Drolet to talk with the group about her efforts in this
area.
