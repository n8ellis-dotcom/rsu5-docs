Agenda Items and Discussion:
3-5 Year Financial Plan:
Director of Finance presented the fourth draft of the 3-5 Year Financial Plan. The revision
incorporated the suggestions made at the October 12, 2011 meeting. Based on early budget
discussions and reflection on the past three budget cycles the Committee felt that the percent of
increase between the years could be reduced from 3% each year to 1%, 1.5%, and 1.5%.
Director of Finance will make necessary revisions for the January 11, 2012 Finance meeting.
