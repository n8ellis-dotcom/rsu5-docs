Agenda Items and Discussion:
Unaudited wrap up of 2010/2011 School Year Financials:
The Director of Finance reviewed financial summary sheet handed out to the Committee. The
current estimate of unexpended funds remaining from the 2010/2011 school year is
approximately $800,000. Kelly also reviewed the Summary of Undesignated Fund Balance
Handout that had been previously presented to the Committee earlier in the year. This summary
identified a target fund balance that was necessary to retain the level of current revenues for next
year budget cycle. The $800,000 is close to the target range necessary to do this. The Auditors
will be in the third week of September to begin the audit process and a final review will be
presented to the Committee when completed.
