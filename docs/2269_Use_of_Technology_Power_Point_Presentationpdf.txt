Technology Workshop
RSU No. 5
November 9, 2011

The RSU No. 5 Strategic Framework includes an academic program commitment to use technology
effectively to enhance learning. The description provided within the framework is as follows:
“
There is little doubt that future success in life is – and will continue to be – tightly tied to adept
use of technology. We are committed to pursuing technology that broadens and deepens student
learning, that increases student responsibility for learning and that prepares them for work
and/or college. To meet these goals, we will need to help our educators learn how best to
graduate “tech-literate” students, financially support appropriate technology in the classroom
and continually review best practices with the use of technology for life-long learning.”

What are we doing?

What did we do last year?
● Purchased laptops for all elementary teachers
● Redefined technology roles to streamline systems support and
classroom support
● Increased computer technician staffing to 2 full-time employees
and 1 school year employee
● Continued with current computer teacher/integrator staffing ratio.
● Selected one Student Information System, PowerSchool
● Replaced 2 wireless networks, 4 network file servers, and various
network equipment
