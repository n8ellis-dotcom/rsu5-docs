Strategic Planning Advisory Committee
January 23, 2019
Minutes
Committee Members Present:
Becky Foley, Mary Jane McCalmon, Seth Koenig, Valy Steverlynck, Stephanie Paine, Julie
Nickerson, Jonathan Dawson, Cynthia Alexander, Lindsay Sterling
Absent: Rhea Fitzpatrick, Nancy Drolet
Purpose of the meeting was to discuss feedback from the Board. The Board approved the first
reading. Added two commas. Bold the mission and the closing sentence. Based upon the
feedback, the committee modified the first sentence under students and in the closing sentence to
read as follows:
In RSU 5, learners become healthy, empathetic, and responsible citizens who contribute to
their local and global communities. Students are creative problem solvers and critical
thinkers who communicate effectively in an evolving world.
Students graduate as compassionate, honest, and respectful citizens who learn and
contribute to their communities for life.
The committee also reviewed the next steps in the Strategic Planning Process. This includes
needing to develop the Core Beliefs. Teachers will develop the draft and then it will come to the
committee to finalize.
The committee will distribute a survey for staff to fill out around the strengths and weaknesses of
our current programs and systems.
The goal is to create long/short term goals during April.
