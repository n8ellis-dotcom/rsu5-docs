FY 27 BUDGET: SCHOOL BOARD BUDGET PRESENTATION
Building/Department: School Nutrition Department
What key issues and priorities are you trying to address in your proposed budget?
RSU 5’s Nutrition Program budget prioritizes student access to nutritious, appealing meals
that support learning, health, and overall well-being. The proposed budget reflects the
district’s consistently high meal participation rates and the operational costs required to
sustain and improve service across all schools. Key issues addressed include rising food,
labor, and supply costs, as well as the need to maintain adequate staffing and to update
kitchen equipment to meet student needs.
Additionally, the budget prioritizes program stability amid changes or uncertainty in
federal and state meal reimbursement and nutrition policy. The Nutrition Department
remains committed to minimizing financial barriers for families, ensuring equitable meal
access for all students, and maintaining compliance with nutrition standards while
continuing to adapt menus and operations to meet student needs and preferences.
