Strategic Communications Subcommittee Report
Date: 6/10/16
Committee: Strategic Communications
Chair: Candice deCcsipkes
In attendance: Candice Decsipkes, Mike Lafortune, Lindsay Sterling, Valy Steverlynck
Meeting Date: 6/8/16
Agenda Items and Discussion:
1. New website: Seth shared the new website, answered some questions and sent the website to
committee members in order that they could review it and send him feedback.
2. Exit survey: The group made final changes to the draft in order to take it to the board for
review. See the attached survey. The recommendation from the group would be to start using
the survey this year.
3. Exit interviews: The group decided to recommend to the board that we do interviews with
Ed, Mike and Brian, asking them to tell us what RDU5 is doing well and where we could work
on improvement.
4. UPP (Unity and Pride Project): The group discussed the pros and cons of renaming the high
school, making a list to take to the board.
