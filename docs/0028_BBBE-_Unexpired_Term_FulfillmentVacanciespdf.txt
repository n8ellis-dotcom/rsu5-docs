NEPN/NSBA Code: BBBE
UNEXPIRED TERM FULFILLMENT/VACANCIES
Board of Directors
A vacancy shall be declared when the term of office of a Director expires, when a Director
changes residency from the municipality, upon the death of a Director, or when a Director
resigns, or, when a Director is absent without excuse from three (3) consecutive regular Board
meetings. The Board shall notify the municipal officers of the municipalities within RSU No. 5
of a vacancy before the annual town meetings or before the regular municipal election. The
municipal officers shall then select an interim Director to serve until the next election.
