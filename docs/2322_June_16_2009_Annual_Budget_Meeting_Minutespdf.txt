Annual Budget Meeting
June 16, 2009
Freeport High School Gymnasium
Superintendent Welsh officially called to order the first Annual Budget Meeting for Regional School
Unit No. 5 at 6:35 P.M.
Article 1A: To elect James G. Boyles as moderator to preside at the meeting.
(Poissonnier – Morang) Voice Vote all Aye – Motion carries
James G. Boyles was sworn in by Superintendent Welsh.
Mr. Boyles explained non-members of the community could only speak if given permission by
members present. Mr. Boyles asked for voter approval for the following Administrators and
Representative: Diane Boucher, Peter Buckley, Kathie Girard, Joan Nason, Dennis Ouellette,
Russell Packett, Will Pidden, Elaine Tomaszewski, Cheryl White and Rep. Susan Austin. Voice
Vote - Yes
Mr. Boyles explained the process/rules for the meeting:
- Robert’s Rules of Order modified by the Maine Municipal Association will be followed;
- A person may not speak before being recognized by the moderator;
- Speakers must come to the microphone;
- The moderator’s function is to act as a medium – all comments are addressed to Mr. Moderator; and
- An amendment can only specify what amount not a specific item.
