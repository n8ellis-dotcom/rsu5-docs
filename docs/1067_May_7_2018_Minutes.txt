Finance Subcommittee Report
Date: May 9, 2018
Committee: Finance Committee
Chair: Kate Brown
In attendance: Kate Brown, Michelle Ritcheson, Michelle Lickteig
Guests: Peter Joseph, Dr. Becky Foley, John Egan, Eric Horne, Kevin Nadeau, Sarah Tracy, Rich
George, Scott Gleeson, and Tim Giddinge,
Meeting Date: May 7, 2018
Agenda Items and Discussion:
Exchange Information Between Towns
Based on the last meeting’s requests, Peter Joseph of Freeport, brought information on the Freeport
TIF (Tax Increment Financing) programs. Peter explained how each TIF worked and how that
impacts the State Valuation of the town of Freeport. Kevin Nadeau, of Durham, passed out two
pieces of information: 1) the historical information about State Valuations, ED279 Valuations and
student enrollments per town going back to the formation of the RSU and 2) the information about
new construction in Durham. Each town shared their average yearly historical new construction.
Get Feedback from Towns in Reference to Additional Research Items
Tim Giddinge, of Pownal, asked if we could research what formulas throughout the state that may
have caused issues and subsequent changes, so that maybe the RSU doesn’t use a formula that may
not work in the long run, if for example, it may have been too complicated to implement.
Discuss next steps
The group discussed next steps including:
- Have the Finance Committee send a written request to the towns for information and data
needed.
- The towns will send written information to the Finance Committee.
- If a workshop is to be scheduled with the School Board, to please have an invitation to the
towns to speak.
