Minutes
1. Meeting called to order at 5:03 p.m.
2. Capital Planning & Financing Discussion -
a. The Facilities and Operations Committee and the Finance Committee continued their
review and discussion the following proposals:
i. Vehicle and Grounds Equipment Replacement
ii. Energy Audit Phases
iii. Life Safety
b. Committee members continued their review and discussion of the proposed funding
for the above projects which includes the use of fund balance to help stabilize the
impact to taxation over the next five budget cycles.
3. The Joint Committee members were in support of moving the project and funding proposals
forward to the full Board for consideration during the upcoming FY26 budget process.
