Agreed upon Norms
● Trust collective wisdom and give some time and thought to ideas before passing
judgment.
● Be conscious of your own and others’ assumptions, and always go to the source to
seek accurate information.
● Assume good intentions, especially during the busiest and most stressful times.
Intentions do not negate impact.
● Maintain confidentiality concerning students, educators, and families with whom we
work.
● Express a Growth Mindset. We have to support our learning by accepting and
encouraging each other to learn from mistakes as well as from success. Do not try and
avoid the discomfort around difficult conversations.
● Seek out and center the participation or experiences of those most impacted by the
topic at hand.

2. FHS Equity Work
a. Principal Gulko and Freeport High School teacher Jessica Najera shared the
work that is being done by the Freeport High School Equity Team.
1. Barriers for Students - The team is examining “hidden costs” associated
with courses and clubs that may be a barrier to participation.
2. Enhancing Student Voice - The Student Leadership Team is focusing on
peer to peer respect and examining who’s voice is not being heard.
3. Professional Development - Teachers have had professional development
about student microaggressions and their response. They continue to
work on looking at curriculum, instructional materials, and instructional
practices through the lens of equity.
3. Framing the Work
a. Agreed upon definitions for diversity, equity, inclusion and belonging - Making
them front facing for the committee
b. Strategic Planning Process
1. Identifying marginalized groups so all voices are at the table
2. Focus Group for the process
