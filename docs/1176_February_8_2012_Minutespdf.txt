Finance Subcommittee Report
Date: February 21, 2012
Committee: Finance Subcommittee
Chair: Eric Dube
Meeting Date: February 8, 2012
Members Present: Eric Dube, John Ricker, John Morang and Shannon Welsh
Agenda Items and Discussion:
RSU 5 Member Communities Meeting to discuss Financial Health/Budget Priorities:
Members of the Finance Committee discussed the structure of the meeting. Review 3-5 year plan
and then review the budget priorities approved by the Board of Directors. The members felt that
one meeting would be ideal so that each community would have the opportunity to hear the
questions and answers that may arise from the discussion. It was suggested to send a personal
invitation to each Board/Council Member at their home address as well as the
Manager/Administrative Assistant via Town email. The date of March 14, 2012 from 6-7 PM
was suggested to be considered by the Board of Directors at their business meeting February 8,
2012.
CBEA Health Insurance Exploration Committee:
Kelly reported that the CBEA Superintendents and the Business Managers/Directors had met to
discuss the formation of a Health Insurance Exploration Group. Recent legislation passed allows
for districts to receive their health insurance loss ratings. This would allow for a competitive
RFP to be sent out. Members of the CBEA would explore the feasibility of this and report
findings. The Finance Committee was in support of this and asked to have it put on the next
Board of Directors meeting agenda on February 29, 2012.
Audit Review:
Kelly reported that the audit had been completed that day and the preliminary reports had been
approved. The auditors and Kelly had spent extra time this year reviewing Adult and
Community Education and recommendations have been implemented to improve the financial
reporting of this department.
Revised ED279:
Kelly reported that the Department of Education had released a revised preliminary ED279. This
revision has an increase in State Aid to Education over 11/12 of $317,081. These numbers will
be used in the creation of the 12/13 budget but may be revised as the Legislature works to
balance the budget.

Warrant Signing:
Warrants were signed.
