Questions will focus on 1,2, 3,4,5,8.
Debrief from the Community Forum on October 2, 2018:
• Standards based grading did not take a lot of air time.
• There were 32 who attended the Community Forum.
o 1 Facilitator
o 18 Parents (3 of these were Committee Members)
o 6 Board members
o 5 Administrators
o 1 Reporter
o 1 Student
• Committee had hoped for more people to participate as there were only 18 parents, and
of those 3 were committee members.
• Some table groups found it hard to sift through data in a coherent way, but did come up
with some good conclusions. One group did get bogged down in the grading system.
• Groups liked the descending order of the data as it made it easier to interpret the data.
• Some individuals were looking at the data through their own agenda, but most did not do
this.
• There did seem to be some common themes across tables such as the desire for
personalized learning.
All notes from both forums will be compiled for our next meeting in November. We will begin to
work on the strategic vision: what are the big ideas? What do people value if the RSU is the
best that it can be?
Lindsay, Seth, and Becky will work together to create a first draft of the document, and then will
bring it back to the committee for editing.
