Bonding Representative:
A Bonding Representative from Moors & Cabot came and spoke to the Finance Committee
about the bonding process. Moors and Cabot was established in 1890 and in 1998 established a
Banking and Advisory Group which provides consultative and advisory services to local
municipal entities for debt management, fiscal advice, and access to debt markets. Moors &
Cabot Banking Advisory Group has worked with several neighboring districts and Towns. The
representative explained the difference between working with the Maine Municipal Bond Bank
and selling our own bonds. He also talked about the benefits of getting our own rating and
shopping the interest rates to take advantage of the lowest rates possible. Moors & Cabot would
be willing to work with the Finance Committee, Director of Finance, and the Superintendent
once the Board decides whether or not to send the project to the voters. This vote is expected in
January of 2013. The Finance Committee will revisit this in January after the Board has voted.
