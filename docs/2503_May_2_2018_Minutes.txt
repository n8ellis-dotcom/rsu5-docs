Strategic Planning Advisory Committee
Minutes
May 2, 2018
Committee Members Present:
Becky Foley, Rhea Fitzpatrick, Valy Steverlynck, Lindsay Sterling, Stephanie Paine, Jonathan
Dawson, Cynthia Alexander, Nancy Drolet,
Facilitator: Mary Jane McCalmon
Reviewed the engagement strategies to ensure that people are filling out the survey.
Lindsay will create business cards to distribute with the website address for the survey.
Lindsay connected with the Chambers of Commerce and they included information about the
survey in their newsletter. Lindsay is also attending the Elders’ Community Center to promote
filling out the survey. Lindsay attended the latest Freeport Council Meeting to inform people
about the survey.
The Y has “Cards and Coffees.” Stephanie will check with them to see if she can attend and
promote filling out the survey.
Nancy and Jonathan will be at the annual budget meeting to distribute the business cards with
the survey information on them. Lindsay will distribute the cards at the Track & Field grand
opening.
Rhea will reach out to the Interact Club to see if the students will distribute cards at the June
12th election in all three towns. Rhea will check with the Club on Friday to check on their
willingness to participate.
Mary Jane feels that we are doing a good job of getting the word out about the survey.
The committee reviewed the agenda for the public forums that will begin this evening at
Freeport High School and the materials that will be needed.
Our next meeting will be Wednesday, June 6, 2018 at 7:30 am.
