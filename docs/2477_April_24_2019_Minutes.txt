The group began to work on the major areas of focus for the work to be included in the strategic
plan. There was discussion around the following focus areas: school climate/culture, instruction,
curriculum/programming, and staff. Focus area discussion will continue at the next meeting and
then the group will discuss which areas will be chosen for the strategic plan.
