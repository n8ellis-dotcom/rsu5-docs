NEPN/NSBA Code: ACA
RESPECTFUL LANGUAGE
RSU No. 5 supports a respectful and inclusive learning and working environment.
In written and verbal communications, all staff should:
• Avoid the use of gendered pronouns when unnecessary (i.e; ‘they’ instead of ‘he/she’)
• Utilize preferred pronouns to address students and colleagues, when known, and
• Refrain from the use of sexist and discriminatory language.
