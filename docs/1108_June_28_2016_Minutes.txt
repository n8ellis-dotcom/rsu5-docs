Finance Subcommittee Report
Date: November 30, 2016
Committee: Finance Committee
Chair: John Morang
In attendance: John Morang, Michelle Ritcheson and Edward R. McDonough
Guests: None
Meeting Date: June 28, 2016
Agenda Items and Discussion:
2016/2017 Capital Reserves Request for funding-Netting System for FHS Baseball Field:
Finance Committee members discussed an email from Superintendent McDonough in regards to a
safety issue at MSS playground area. Stray baseballs occasionally make their way to the
playground at MSS. Athletic Director Sickels had received three quotes on a netting system. The
Finance Committee reviewed the three quotes. John Morang made a motion to expend up to
$22,425 from Capital Reserve Fund for the purchase of a netting system for Freeport High School
Baseball field. Second by Michelle Ritcheson. All voted in favor.
Finance Transfer:
Finance Committee signed Transfer Warrant #2 for a total of $78,069.
2016/2017 Warrant Schedule:
The warrant schedule was reviewed and approved.
