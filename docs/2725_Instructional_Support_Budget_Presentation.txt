FY 26 BUDGET: SCHOOL BOARD BUDGET WORKSHOP PRESENTATION
Building/Department: Instructional Support
What key issues and priorities are you trying to address in your proposed budget?
Due to a decline in enrollment within the Multilingual Learner population, the Office of
Instructional Support is proposing the elimination of one ESOL teaching position. From
October 1, 2023, to October 1, 2024, the Multilingual Learner population has decreased
by 70 students.
The department is not recommending the addition of the one-year special education
educator position that was funded in FY25 through the Article 2 reserve account to the
FY26 budget. Student caseloads for special education staff will be adjusted
accordingly.
