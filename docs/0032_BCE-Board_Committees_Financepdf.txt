NEPN/NSBA Code: BCE
BOARD FINANCE COMMITTEE
The Finance Committee shall consist of three (3) Board members: one (1) from Durham; one (1)
from Freeport; and one (1) from Pownal; effective July 1, 2009. Such members are to be elected
by the Board annually in June to serve for the fiscal year starting July 1 or as needed based upon
municipal elections.
