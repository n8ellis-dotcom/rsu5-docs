Agenda Items and Discussion:
Meeting called to order at 5:04pm
1. FHS Tennis Courts Update- Director Reynolds updated the Committee that the site
survey is underway and the core sampling would be happening in the next few weeks..
Director Reynolds went over the preliminary layouts of the current 3 court and a
possible 4 court replacement options. The Committee then reviewed and discussed the
early concept estimates for both asphalt and post tension courts. Tennis Coach, Molly
Meyers, gave an update on additional steps needed during the USTA grant application
process which include a technical review of the original set of drawings and a final
review of all bid documents by USTA before bidding can commence. This process would
have implications on the timeline. Director Reynolds was asked to provide additional
information on the layout of the courts as well as the possibility of a curtain instead of
fencing between the courts.
2. Energy Audit Phase 1 Project Update- Director Reynolds updated the Committee that the
lighting vendor is currently working at Freepoort Middle School. The next location for
this project is Mast Landing School. Director Reynolds also shared that once the lighting
project at Mast Landing is complete the vendor will be going to Freeport High School.
This is a change from the original schedule due to the length of time the lighting project
will take and the availability of the FPAC. Director Reynolds also reported that EMC was
preparing an updated Phase 2 Projects Listing.
3. Facility Study Update- Director Reynolds shared with the Committee a draft of the
proposed Facility Study Request for Qualifications (RFQ) document. Superintendent Tom
Gray and Director of Finance and Human Resources Kelly Wentworth shared the overall
concept of the Facility Study process. This would begin with a review of all current

facilities, a determination of current conditions, an educational analysis of needs, a
demographic/enrollment study, Community feedback listening sessions, then suggest
and rank possible space reuse to meet educational and economic conditions in the next
ten years. Committee Member Karen Breer shared that she has access to some other
Facility Study RFQs and would email them to Superintendent Gray who can then share
them with the Committee. Committee members are asked to share any suggested
revisions and/or changes prior to the next meeting so that the Committee can review
any revisions to the RFQ at the next meeting on April 8, 2026.
Motion to adjourn was made by Michelle Ritcheson second by Karen Breer at 6:13 p.m.
