Agenda Items and Discussion:
State of the Schools Draft Report
Joe Makley shared preliminary copies of the State of the School report. On the NECAP, RSU5
students are continuing to perform well above the state average and approaching regional
averages in reading and math. Both 3rd and 5th grade students matched or exceeded the regional
average in math. Grade 8 students performed well against the state average, but somewhat below
the regional average in math. On the Maine High School Assessment, RSU5 students continue
to score well above the state averages in all subjects, and somewhat below the regional average,
with the largest gap in math. (Secondary math is an improvement focus at RSU5.) The number
of low-income students at RSU5 increased slightly in 2013, and remains more than double that
of the higher scoring regional schools.
