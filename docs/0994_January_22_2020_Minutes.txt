Finance Subcommittee Report
Date: January 29, 2020
Committee: Finance Committee
Chair: Kate Brown
In attendance: Kate Brown, Jeremy Clough, Michelle Ritcheson, Becky Foley, Ann Cromer
Guests: Elisabeth Munsen
Meeting Date: January 22, 2020
Agenda Items and Discussion:
Sign Warrants:
Accounts Payable Warrants were signed.
Discuss work plan of the Committee for the remainder of the year:
The Committee reviewed a timeline from a prior year and discussed items which could be addressed this
year, and what should wait until next year.
Brainstorm ideas for cost sharing:
The Committee members shared their latest thinking. Beth gave feedback from her perspective on what the
Board could benefit from understanding more clearly about the cost sharing methods the Committee
continues to see as viable options.
Additional Item:
The Committee discussed the remaining balance in the High School Renovation Fund, what its origins might
have been, and restrictions on its use. Ann agreed to review journal entries or any other activity in the
financial system that would explain the balance.
