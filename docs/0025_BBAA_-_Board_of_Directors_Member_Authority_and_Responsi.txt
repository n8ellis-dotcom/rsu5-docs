NEPN/NSBA Code: BBBA
BOARD OF DIRECTORS MEMBER QUALIFICATIONS
A person is legally qualified to become a member of the Board of Directors if they are a United
States citizen, a resident of the State of Maine, a qualified voter in the community or ward thereof
by and from which they are elected and at least 18 years of age.
Elections shall be nonpartisan. Candidates’ qualifications, residency requirements and
proper nomination procedures shall be the same as for candidates for city or town Council.
No member of the Board of Directors or spouse shall be an employee in any public school within
the system.
