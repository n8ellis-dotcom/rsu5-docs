Guests: Erica Wallstrom,
An overview of the year-long curriculum work in science at the high school was presented to the
committee. The presentation outlined our goals as a high school science department:
developing a common definition and understanding of phenomena and phenomena-based
learning, and alignment to the Next Generation Science Standards.
The outcomes this year have focused on collective learning and growth, implementing
phenomena-based instruction, and focusing on the cross-cutting standards and science
practices. These practices will have a feedback process that is being developed.
