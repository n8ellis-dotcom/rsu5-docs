Agenda Items and Discussion:
State of the Schools Report:
Joe Makley, Curriculum Director, joined the group to review the first proof of our 2nd annual State of the
Schools report. Committee members discussed both the content and presentation of the document, making
suggested revisions. We anticipate the report will be ready for distribution to all household in our RSU5
community beginning Thursday, March 22nd. Additional copies of the report have been ordered to distribute to
all staff and to the Board in advance of the mailing. Committee members thanked Joe for his efforts on
compiling the data for this report.
Education Forum Planning:
Commissioner Bowen has agreed to kick-off our Community Forum, scheduled for April 2, 2012. The
committee created the following agenda for that evening’s presentation and discussion:
• Welcome and Introduction by Chair Larkins
• Keynote Speech by Commissioner Bowen
• State of Schools Summary by Supt. Welsh
• Staff Presentations of our current educational initiatives
• Brief Summary and Recognition of Efforts by Supt. Welsh
• Facilitated Q&A/Discussion Session by Chair Larkins
The Forum will be advertised through school newsletters; Delivering; Cable TV; Parent e-mail list; a press
release; and an ad in local papers. There is also an invitation in the State of the Schools Report that is being
mailed to each household in the RSU. We are all hopeful that this format will educate and engage our
community members.
